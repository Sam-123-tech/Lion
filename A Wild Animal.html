LION

The lion is a wild animal. Lion is the king of the jungle. Lion is a carnivore animal. It only hunts when it is hungry. The Gir National park is famous for lions. The lion likes to eat the flesh of other animals. The Lion is the national animal of Srilanka. The male lion has a mane around its neck while the female one has no mane. The female lion is called a lioness. It lives in a den. A lion's sound is called roar. Their babies are called cubs. Its a very brave an a majestic animal.
